Learn How to Report on Instagram

Follow these steps to properly report an account that's violating Instagram's policies

1

Go to the Profile

Navigate to the Instagram profile you want to report. Click the three dots (•••) in the top right corner of their profile.

2

Select 'Report'

From the menu that appears, select "Report". You'll be given several options for why you're reporting the account.

3

Choose Report Reason

Select the most appropriate reason for your report. For privacy violations, choose "It's posting content that shouldn't be on Instagram" or "It's pretending to be me or someone else".

4

Provide Details

Follow the prompts to provide additional information about the violation. Be specific and accurate in your description.

5

Submit and Wait

Submit your report. Instagram will review it and take action if the account violates their policies. This process can take anywhere from a few hours to several days.

Pro Tip from Awaken

For impersonation accounts, make sure to report them as "Pretending to be me" specifically. This category gets prioritized in Instagram's review system.

Instagram Safety Guide

Protect your account from hackers, impersonators, and privacy violations

Strong Password

Use a unique, complex password for your Instagram account that you don't use anywhere else.

Two-Factor Authentication

Enable two-factor authentication for an extra layer of security on your account.

Private Account

Consider setting your account to private to control who can see your content.

Be Careful What You Share

Avoid posting sensitive personal information that could be used to impersonate you.

Check Third-Party Apps

Regularly review and remove any third-party apps that have access to your Instagram account.

Monitor Login Activity

Regularly check your login activity to spot any unauthorized access to your account.
